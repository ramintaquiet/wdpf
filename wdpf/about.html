Our Story

The Kind of Blu Café is a perfect coffee spot in central Dublin. It’s located on the river Liffey’s south bank, between Tara Station and O’Connell Bridge. It’s a full working cafe, and a hive of activity. A popular spot for breakfast meetings, casual lunches and get-togethers. We have a Deli Bar with a variety selection of sandwiches for breakfast and lunch. We serve Italian brand Lavazza coffee to hundreds of customers every day. Within a few decades, coffee breaks had become a workplace ritual. Tradition of gossiping around mugs of coffee had been adopted by the Irish culture. Stopping to swallow a gulp of coffee isn’t a waste of time—instead, it could actually nurture professional relationships. The Kind of Blu café is a perfect location for a business meeting or a quick lunch break.
